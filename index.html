BALLROOM AND LATIN DANCE CLASSES

Ballroom and Latin Dance Classes for Adult Beginners and Improvers. Covering Sandy, Biggleswade, Potton and surrounding areas.
Learn to dance the Social Foxtrot, Waltz and Quickstep Ballroom Dances and Jive, Cha Cha Cha and Rumba Latin Dances among others.

Beginners Classes run on Monday evenings 7:30 to 9:30 at Sutton Village Hall, 1A High Street, Sutton, Sandy, Bedfordshire, SG19 2NE

Improvers Classes run on Tuesday evenings 7:30 to 9:30 at Sandy Conservative Club, 19 Bedford Road, Sandy, SG19 1EL

Why enrol in our classes?

Dancing is great for: health, posture, increased self-esteem improved mobility and coordination, improved fitness and stamina. But most of all, it is really great fun!

Ballroom and Latin Dance Classes:

The Dances

In Ballroom you will Learn to dance Social Foxtrot, Waltz and Quickstep at beginners level.

Add more figures and style at Improvers Level and add other dances such as Viennese Waltz, Ballroom Tango and Sequence Dances

In Latin you will Learn to dance Jive, Cha Cha Cha and Rumba at beginners level.

Add more figures and style at Improvers Level and introduce other dances like Paso Doble and Argentine Tango

Social Dancing

We run regular Social dances to give you the opprotunity to practice what you have learned and enjoy the social side of dancing. Keep an eye on our social media, email newsletter and the social diary at class for dates and details.

Private Lessons

Private Lessons for couples or small groups can be arranged. Choreography with tuition is also available for Weddings and other Special Occasions. Contact Us for more information and bookings.
