About us and our DANCE lessons

Meet The Team:

Les Durham

Les is the Lead Instructor and business owner of Learn to Dance Sandy. He is a fully qualified instructor with the International Dance Teachers Association.

He is a fully qualified instructor of many years standing. He started dancing in 1986 going through the medal training system and qualified with the International Dance Teachers Association in 1997.

Les started teaching in schools in North London before going independent after a year. Since then he has taught in various locations in Hertfordshire, Bedfordshire and Cyprus.

Clive Reynolds

Assistant Instructor and Demo.

Clive and his wife Dawn have honed their dance skills over many years under Les' guidance. They enjoy their dancing, both at lessons and at social events. They also enjoy helping other people to flourish as dancers in their own right.

Dawn Reynolds

Assistant Instructor and Demo.

Just Like Clive, Dawn has been dancing for many years, enjoys dancing at classes and social events. Dawn thoroughly enjoys passing on that experience to others.
